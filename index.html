How It Works

Set Limits

Choose daily caps for distracting apps or overall phone time. Create schedules for work, rest, and off-hours. THOUGHBOX enforces your rules consistently.

Focus Sessions

Start a timed focus session. During the session, your phone use is blocked or reduced to essential apps only. Ideal for deep work, reading, and intentional breaks.

Smart Lockouts

When a limit is reached, access is locked for a cooling period. Emergency bypass is available for calls and truly urgent needs, with transparent logging.

Progress Tracking

See daily and weekly summaries of your time saved and sessions completed. Celebrate streaks and measure improvements to stay motivated.
